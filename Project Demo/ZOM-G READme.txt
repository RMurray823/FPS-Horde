ZOM-G DEMO

CONTROLS:

movement:
W - forward
A - strafe left
S - backward
D - strafe right

menu:
esc - opens/closes pause menu

weapon:

left mouse - fire (hold down for automatic fire)
r - reload weapon
tab - switch weapon


WEAPONS:

light rifle - low damage high fire rate
heavy rifle - high damage low fire rate

ITEMS:

green - health
purple - armor
blue - ammo (goes to currently selected weapon)

Download the ZOM-G Demo_v2 folder, and open FPS-Horde.exe this will open a Unity settings panel, select appropriate settings, click play and enjoy!
